With the mock-ups in hand, I meticulously crafted a new WordPress theme, ensuring seamless alignment with the designs. Rigorous testing across various browsers and devices has been completed, confirming its readiness for public consumption.

I refined my expertise in crafting custom WordPress themes over the years. After a brief hiatus, I was recently commissioned to create another custom WordPress theme.

Tech-Stack: HTML, CSS/Sass, JavaScript, PHP, and the robust WordPress CMS (content management system).
